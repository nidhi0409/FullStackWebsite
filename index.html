About Us


The Books Loft was born in 2022 as a publishing consultant company, a first of its kind in India. With a strong focus on strategy, marketing and communication for the publishing sector, The Books Loft became widely known due to its TheBooksLoft.com website. After just a few time, the site turned into a newsletter, averaging 3,000 weekly viewers. The Books Loft aimed at keeping the Worldwide professional market informed by editing and publishing important works such as Book Publishing and Strategic Management, by Nidhi Chauhan, and since 2022 “Publishing Personalities”
